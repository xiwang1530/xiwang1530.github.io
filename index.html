Xi Wang

Research Fellow in Robotics
Rolls-Royce University Technology Centre in Manufacturing & On-Wing Technology
University of Nottingham, UK

I am a Research Fellow at the Rolls-Royce University Technology Centre (UTC) in Manufacturing & On-Wing Technology, University of Nottingham, where I also completed my PhD in Manufacturing Engineering in 2024. My research develops small-scale soft and continuum robots for inspection and repair in extremely confined spaces — such as the sub-millimetre gaps inside aero-engines and the hazardous interiors of nuclear facilities.

My work spans flexible actuation, structural and topology optimisation, rigid–flexible coupling, flexible / tactile sensing, and learning-based control. My long-term goal is to build the next generation of automated, intelligent, millimetre-to-centimetre-scale robots that can navigate, perceive and make decisions inside high-value assets — making inspection and maintenance safer, cheaper and greener.
